Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

ConviSize is a client-side image conversion tool that processes images locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or image data to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since ConviSize operates entirely in your browser:

  • Your images are processed locally on your device
  • No image data is uploaded to our servers
  • No personal information is collected or stored
  • No cookies are used for tracking purposes

3. Data Storage

All image processing occurs locally in your browser's memory. No data is permanently stored on our servers or transmitted over the internet.

4. Third-Party Services

ConviSize may use third-party libraries for image processing functionality. These libraries operate locally in your browser and do not transmit data externally.

5. Security

Since all processing happens locally on your device, your images and data remain secure and private. We recommend using the latest version of your browser for optimal security.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact Us page.
